A quick intro

Hi, I'm Miriam! About 25 years ago I was a Webmaster. I built web pages (in HTML), developed the graphics, did some fancy layout (with a single-pixel gif trick), hosted the Apache web server on my desktop, looked after the domains and supported users. After that, I went onto do a good deal in audio broadcating online and continued advancing the designs and development of websites as newer and newer technologies emerged. I've worked at all levels including management, UX, design, mobile development and more.

The area that appeals to me most is the overall design of a site or an app, including the architecture and the UX/UI. I love simple designs with impecable wording and feel driven to always make it intuitive for people to use. I have a good deal of technical experience and that gives me an edge in design-thinking and creative problem-solving.

The sector or area of work I am involved in is very important to me and I like to have my values aligned with the work that I do: This means using technology with the perfect design to help people lead better lives. The sectors I like to work in are: health, education and media.
